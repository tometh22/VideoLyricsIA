03 — AI Usage Scope

AI = Backgrounds Only

AI-Generated

Background visuals only — landscapes, abstract scenes, atmospheric effects. No people, faces, characters, or main content. Must be disclaimed for US copyright registration.

Human-Created

Lyrics overlay & timing, typography selection, visual composition, audio-visual synchronization, style choices, original audio. All copyrightable.

What AI does NOT do

No people / faces / hands generation

No lip-sync or face-swap

No likeness regeneration

No audio generation or modification

No existing footage extension

No full scene or character creation

03 — AI Usage Scope (continued)

Human-Created Background Option

Users can upload their own background assets, bypassing AI generation entirely. This option enables workflows where AI modifies or composes over human-created content rather than generating from scratch.

Upload Custom Background

MP4, MOV, JPG, or PNG. AI background generation is completely skipped. Provenance records the asset as "human-provided".

Full Copyright Retention

When using human-provided backgrounds, the entire visual composition consists of human-created elements — fully copyrightable.

05 — Content Safety

Three-Layer Content Protection

1

Prompt Blocking

All generation prompts include explicit exclusion of people, faces, hands, text, logos, and trademarks. Enforced at code level — cannot be overridden by users.

"No people, no faces,
no hands, no text,
no words, no letters"
2

AI Validation

After generation, video frames are extracted and analyzed by Gemini Vision to detect prohibited content. If violations are found, the job is automatically blocked.

Blocked automatically if people, faces, text, or logos detected

3

Human Review

Every job requires explicit human approval. Downloads and YouTube publication are blocked until a reviewer approves. Identity and timestamp logged.

Approve / Reject with notes

06 — Data Protection

No Training on Client Data

Google Cloud Vertex AI Enterprise does not use customer data to train foundation models. Per Google Cloud's Terms of Service and Data Processing Amendment (DPA), customer data is processed in real-time and not retained beyond the API request lifecycle. GenLy AI does not opt in to any training programs and does not perform fine-tuning on any models.

Data sent to each API — minimized by design

APIData SentNOT Sent
Gemini (Lyrics) First 600 chars of lyrics, artist name* Full audio, full lyrics, user PII
Veo 3.1 (Video) Scene prompt only Audio, lyrics, artist name
Imagen 4 (Image) Scene prompt only Audio, lyrics, artist name
Gemini (YouTube) Artist, song, 300 chars lyrics Full audio, user PII
Gemini Vision Video frames (images only) Audio, lyrics, artist name

* Artist name is configurable — can be anonymized via platform settings.

Audio files are never sent to any external AI service. Whisper transcription runs locally.

07 — Authorization & Clearance

Pre-Authorization + Mandatory Review

User Pre-Authorization

All users must be explicitly authorized by a platform administrator before accessing AI generation features. Unauthorized users receive a 403 Forbidden response. Full audit trail of who authorized whom and when.

Mandatory Human Clearance

Every generated video enters "Pending Review" status. A human reviewer must examine video, short, and thumbnail before approving. Downloads and YouTube publication are blocked until explicit approval. Reviewer identity and notes logged.

Workflow

Upload MP3 → AI Generation → Content Validation → Pending Review → Human Approval → Available
